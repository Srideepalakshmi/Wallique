# Wallique V3 – The Emotional Wall Calendar

Wallique V3 is a premium interactive wall calendar designed to create a unique emotional connection with every month of the year. Moving beyond static utility, Wallique transforms into a new visual identity every month, matching the seasonal mood, celebrations, and atmosphere of the current time.

![Wallique V3 Preview](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1490750967868-88aa4486c946?auto=format&fit=crop&q=80&w=2000)

## 🎨 Monthly Visual Identities

Wallique automatically updates its entire design system based on the selected month:

- **January**: Cool Blue & Silver — New Year Celebration.
- **February**: Soft Pink — Romantic Floral Mood.
- **March**: Fresh Green — Spring Rebirth.
- **April**: Bright Pastels — Blossom Season.
- **May**: Summer Sun — Golden Light.
- **June**: Nature Teal — Fresh Rain & Growth.
- **July**: Sky Blue — High Energy Summers.
- **August**: Festive Red — Celebration Tones.
- **September**: Earthy Brown — Harvest Mood.
- **October**: Autumn Orange — Falling Leaves.
- **November**: Golden Glow — Festival of Lights.
- **December**: Winter Green — Festive Year-End.

## 💎 Premium Features

- **Dynamic Theme Engine**: Hero images, primary colors, accents, and background atmospheric lights update automatically with fluid transitions.
- **Interactive Wall Grid**: 
  - **Tactile Responses**: Hover glows and click-scale effects.
  - **Smart Selection**: Multi-click range selection with distinct "Anchor" and "Middle" styling.
  - **Seasonal Markers**: Integrated holiday badges that match the month’s palette.
- **The Narrative Journal**:
  - **Contextual Context**: The journal header changes its tone and content based on your selected dates.
  - **Persistent Registry**: Your entries are securely stored in `localStorage`, maintaining your year-long narrative.
  - **Premium Typography**: Using high-contrast, black-weight fonts for a modern product feel.

## 🛠️ Technical Maturity

- **React 19 & Next.js 16**: Utilizing the latest concurrent rendering features.
- **Framer Motion 11**: Orchestrating complex layout animations and theme interpolations.
- **Zero-Hydration Mismatch**: Clean client-side initialization for reliable performance.
- **Responsive Architecture**: Engineered to feel like a desktop workspace that gracefully collapses into a mobile narrative.

## 🏃‍♂️ Setup

1. `npm install`
2. `npm run dev`
3. Navigate to `http://localhost:3000`
